.. py:function:: laplace_inverse_cdf(u, b, p, prec)

   Returns inverse CDF for Lap(u,b) at p.

   :param u: The mean of the distribution. Must be finite and non-nan.
   :param b: The scale of the distribution. Must be finite, non-nan and non-negative.
   :param p: Probability to compute the CDF at.
   :param prec: Precision to use for computing CDF.



.. py:function:: laplace(u, b, step_size = 63)

   Samples a float from the Laplace distribution.

   :param u: The mean of the distribution. Must be finite and non-nan.
   :param b: The scale of the distribution. Must be positive, finite and non-nan.
   :param step_size: How many bits of probability to sample at a time.
