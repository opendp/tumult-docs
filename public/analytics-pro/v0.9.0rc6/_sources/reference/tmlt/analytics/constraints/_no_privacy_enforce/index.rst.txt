.. py:function:: change_metric_without_enforcing_constraint(constraint, child_transformation, child_ref, **kwargs)

   Non-private version of Constraint._enforce.

   Enforcing constraints in non-private measurements is unnecessary because the second
   application of a constraint is just for the stability analysis, but when the
   privacy budget is infinite, the stability can be infinite anyway.

   Instead, the constraints are enforced according to `enforce_constraints` in the
   `NoPrivacyTransformationVisitor.visit_enforce` class.
